Privacy Policy

Effective: June 30, 2026 · ArchRavels Digital (Beta)
This is an initial version for the ArchRavels Digital beta and may be updated as the app evolves. Questions? Email info@xyzgamelabs.com.

XYZ Game Labs ("we," "us," "our") makes ArchRavels Digital (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App handles and how. We aim to collect as little as possible.

Information we handle

Account & sign-in. If you choose to sign in (with Google, Apple, or an email magic link), our authentication provider receives your email address and a unique account identifier so we can save your progress. You can use most of the App as a guest without signing in.

Game progress. If signed in, we store your gameplay data — such as match results, achievements, and unlocked content — so it syncs across your devices.

Email list (optional). If you submit your email to our updates list, we use it only to send news about ArchRavels Digital. You can unsubscribe at any time.

Diagnostics. The App and its hosting may process basic technical data (such as device type or error logs) to keep the App working. We do not use this to identify you personally.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not show third-party advertising in the App.
  • We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Service providers

We rely on trusted providers to run the App, including authentication and database hosting (Supabase), web hosting (GitHub Pages), and email (Mailchimp). These providers process data on our behalf under their own terms.

Your choices

You can play as a guest, sign out at any time, unsubscribe from emails, or ask us to delete your account data by emailing info@xyzgamelabs.com.

Changes

We may update this policy as the App grows. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email info@xyzgamelabs.com.
